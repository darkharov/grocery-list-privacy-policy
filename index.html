Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18.02.2026
This Privacy Policy describes how Grocery List on Lock Screen ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Android application (the "App").
By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Analytics Data


We use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to collect information about how users interact with the App. This helps us understand usage patterns and improve the functionality and performance of the App.
The analytics data may include:
  • App usage events (such as screen views, button interactions, and feature usage)
  • Device-related information (such as device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address (used for approximate location and technical purposes)
This data does not include information that directly identifies you, such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Advertising Identifier

The App may collect and use the Advertising ID provided by your device. The Advertising ID is used for analytics and measurement purposes only and is not used to directly identify you as an individual.
You can reset or limit the use of the Advertising ID at any time through your device settings.

3. Local Data Storage

The App uses a local database on your device to store app-related data necessary for the App's functionality. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
  • Analyze how the App is used
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and improve stability
  • Send push notifications (if enabled)

Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.
However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information as described in their own privacy policies, including:
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
These third-party services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data.
